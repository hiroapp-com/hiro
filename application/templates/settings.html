About
Hiro is for people with big ideas
but insufficient tools to track them.
Like many others we where wondering: Why do we still have so many textfiles on our desktop, or send emails to ourselves? Sure, there are plenty of apps to write longer texts or create colorful scrapbooks. But we wanted something incredibly simple, fast, that works on any device, without complicated syncing through third party services. Something you open when a thought you don't want to lose crosses your mind. Not a todo list. Something that just works, without 3 taps or 4 menus for each action.

We hope you like Hiro as much as we do. We're always there at hello@hiroapp.com and share the best tricks of how people manage their ideas on facebook.com/hiroapp.
