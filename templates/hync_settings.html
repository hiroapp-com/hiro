Hiro is for people with big ideas
who want a better tool to keep them.
Why do we still have so many textfiles on our desktop, or send emails to ourselves? Sure, there are plenty of apps to write longer texts, create colorful scrapbooks or intricate mindmaps. But we wanted something incredibly simple, fast, that works on any device, without complicated syncing through third party services or 3 menus before you can do anything. Something you open when an idea you don't want to lose crosses your mind. A place where it is safe and not accessed by anybody else.

We hope you like Hiro as much as we do. We're always there at hello@hiroapp.com and share the best tricks of how people manage their ideas on facebook.com/hiroapp.
